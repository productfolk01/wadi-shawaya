About Us

Welcome to Wadi Shawaya, where authentic Arabian flavors meet warm hospitality. Located in the heart of Saudi Arabia, Wadi Shawaya is more than just a restaurant — it’s a destination for those who appreciate traditional taste, fresh ingredients, and unforgettable dining experiences.

At Wadi Shawaya, we take pride in preparing every dish with care, using time-honored recipes that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the region. From our signature shawaya grills to our flavorful rice specialties and freshly made sides, every bite is crafted to bring comfort and satisfaction.

Our mission is simple — to serve delicious, high-quality food in a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. Whether you’re dining in with family, ordering for takeaway, or enjoying our meals at your events, Wadi Shawaya promises an experience full of flavor, freshness, and quality.
